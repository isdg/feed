# A ClickHouse-powered Observability Solution: Overview of Highlight.io

Highlight.io is an open-source observability platform that utilizes ClickHouse for data storage and retrieval. Initially focused on session replay and frontend web development features, Highlight.io has now expanded into the full-stack observability space
