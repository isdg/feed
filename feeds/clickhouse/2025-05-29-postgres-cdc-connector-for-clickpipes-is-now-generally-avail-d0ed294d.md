# Postgres CDC connector for ClickPipes is now Generally Available

We’re excited to announce the general availability of the Postgres CDC connector in ClickPipes. Natively integrated with ClickHouse Cloud, this connector enables blazing-fast replication of your Postgres databases with just a few clicks.
