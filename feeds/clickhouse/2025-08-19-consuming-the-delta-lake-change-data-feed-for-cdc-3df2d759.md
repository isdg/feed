# Consuming the Delta Lake Change Data Feed for CDC

In this post, we share everything we have learnt from our investigation into working with Delta Lake’s Change Data Feed (CDF). On top, we’re open sourcing an MIT-licensed, reference implementation of CDC from Delta Lake to ClickHouse in Python.
