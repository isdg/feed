# How Superwall uses WarpStream and ClickHouse Cloud to scale subscription monetization

Superwall powers paywall monetization for billions of end users and ingests 100 MB/sec of events. Using WarpStream (now Confluent) and ClickHouse Cloud, the 14-person team scaled to 300 TB while keeping dashboards fast and operations minimal.
