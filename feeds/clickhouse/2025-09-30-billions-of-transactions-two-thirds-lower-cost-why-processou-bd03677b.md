# Billions of transactions, two-thirds lower cost: Why ProcessOut switched from Elasticsearch to ClickHouse Cloud

ProcessOut replaced a costly, slow, hard-to-change Elasticsearch setup with ClickHouse Cloud, lowering costs by two-thirds and reducing latency from minutes to ~2 seconds.
