# If it’s in your catalog, you can query it: The DataLakeCatalog engine in ClickHouse Cloud

ClickHouse Cloud can now query Iceberg and Delta Lake tables directly through the DataLakeCatalog engine. Connect to Glue or Unity catalogs, discover tables automatically, and query your Lakehouse data instantly, all at ClickHouse speed.
