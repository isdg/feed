# Introducing pg_clickhouse: A Postgres extension for querying ClickHouse

Today, we’re pleased to release pg\_clickhouse, an Apache 2-licensed PostgreSQL extension to transparently execute analytics queries on ClickHouse directly from PostgreSQL.
