# ClickHouse appoints new leader for Asia Pacific and expands global go-to-market leadership team

ClickHouse, a leader in real-time analytics, data warehousing, observability, and AI/ML, today announced an expansion of its global go-to-market (GTM) leadership team, including the appointment of Ed Lenta as Vice President, Asia Pacific and Japan (APJ).
