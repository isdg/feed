# How Visa went from multi-day reporting to conversational analytics agents with ClickHouse Cloud and LibreChat

Visa paired LibreChat with ClickHouse Cloud to build conversational BI agents on Authorize.net payments data, cutting multi-day reports to sub-second queries and reclaiming 8-10 hours per user each week.
