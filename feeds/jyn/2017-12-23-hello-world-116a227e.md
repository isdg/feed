# Hello, World!

**Hello, World!**

This is my first post on the new and improved site using [jekyll](https://github.com/jekyll/jekyll). It will (hopefully) be the first of many. Feel free to explore the source code [here](https://github.com/jyn514/second-website).

I started this site from an offhand comment by my good friend [Charles Daniels](https://github.com/charlesdaniels). We were working on a [side project](https://github.com/charlesdaniels/bitshuffle) together when I suggested signing release commits with [GPG](https://gnupg.org/). He was surprised I had experience with PGP keys, and his first question was "Do you have a blog?"

From that simple question sprung this site. I hope you enjoy!
