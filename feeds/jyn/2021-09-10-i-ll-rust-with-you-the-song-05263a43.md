# I'll Rust With You: the song [Anchor link for: i-ll-rust-with-you-the-song](https://jyn.dev/i-ll-rust-with-you-the-song/\#i-ll-rust-with-you-the-song)

Niko Matsakis, co-lead for the Rust language team, recently published a song titled ["Edition: the
song"](https://smallcultfollowing.com/babysteps/blog/2021/05/26/edition-the-song/). I decided I was not to be outdone and sang a cover of "I'll Rust With You", by [Steam Powered Giraffe](https://steampoweredgiraffe.com/). The song has absolutely no relation to the Rust language other than the title, I just think it's fun.
