# Kernel prepatch 7.2-rc2

The [7.2-rc2](https://lwn.net/Articles/1081366/) kernel prepatch is out for testing. Linus said: "It's Sunday afternoon, and rc2 is out. Things look very normal - it's not a small rc2, but it's in line with recent releases, and slightly smaller than rc2 was in 7.1. Let's see how that all continues, but so far so good."
