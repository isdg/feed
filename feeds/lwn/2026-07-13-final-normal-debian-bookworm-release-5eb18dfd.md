# Final normal Debian bookworm release

Debian has [announced](https://lwn.net/Articles/1082645/) the final normal update for Debian 12 ("bookworm"). Long-term-support updates will [continue until 2028](https://lwn.net/ml/all/a27c47d0f34f36e497d2596a9adfbcb662cd4439.camel%40debian.org/). As may be expected from a stable version, the update is mostly limited to security fixes. Still, it may be time for Debian users to look into upgrading to a more recent version. Conveniently, Debian 13 ("trixie") also [received an update](https://lwn.net/Articles/1082646/) this weekend, with many of the same security fixes.
